Linocut

Coneflowers Rule!

  1. Draw image on linocut block with pencil or pen
  2. Carve with tools
  3. Use a roller to cover the block with ink
  4. Place sheet of printmaking paper over inked image
  5. Rub with large spoon or other hard object to nake sure paper is well covered in ink
  6. Peel paper

What You'll Need:

Speedball Block Printing Starter Kit
