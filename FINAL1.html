Fall Leaves Dinosaur

ROARRRR!

Pick up colorful fallen leaves to create shapes with child. Are they into dinosaurs, dragons, etc.? Channel your creativity with child to produce something adorable and meaningful. Help toddler apply modpodge with a thick brush and you can even frame it.

What You'll Need:

leaves, leaves, leaves!

scissors

cardstock/watercolor paper to glue the leaves on

Optional

stencils

brush

mod podge for framing
